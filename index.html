Hello. My name is Robyn and I'm a problem solver |

I’m also a developer, activist, artist, researcher, mountain-biker, advocate and Glaswegian-turned-Londoner. I’m currently studying BA Product Design at Central Saint Martins, UAL, having left high school in 2015 after studying such subjects as Maths, English, Physics, Graphic Communication, Product Design, and Art & Design. ‘Problem solving’, in my estimation, is the essence of applied design. First and foremost I am a Product Designer and more recently, developer. I believe in following a set of universal principles with all my design-related work and use it as a channel with which to explore topics that interest me such as Politics, sociology, technological development and new skill sets.
