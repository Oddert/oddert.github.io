Higher Graphic Communication Thematic folio

A full thematic portfolio for 50% of Higher Graphic Communication Score.

In sixth year, I crashed Higher Graphic Communication to sharpen my technical competency and visual presentation capability. 50% of this final mark is taken form a thematic portfolio produced throughout the year, based on a selected consumer product.
