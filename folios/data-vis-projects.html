Data Visualisation Projects

The Data Visualisation certification is a (now Legacy) freeCodeCamp certification centred around building data visualisation apps with D3.js.

D3 is a highly powerful set of tools designed to offer a level of control and potential not found in typical ‘chart making’ programs / libraries. As many of these mini projects are quite specific or are built on dummy data, I have collected some here to act as a gallery for data viaualisation.
